Roronoa Zoro is a fictional character in the One Piece manga and anime series created by Eiichiro Oda. He is a skilled swordsman and a member of the Straw Hat Pirates, serving as the crew's swordsman and one of its founding members. Zoro is known for his ambition to become the world's greatest swordsman, a goal he pursues with unwavering determination.
